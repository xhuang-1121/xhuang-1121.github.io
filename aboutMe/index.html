Dr. Xingjun Huang, Management Science and Engineering, Chongqing University

My research area is energy economics and sustainable consumption with a focus on technology adoption and diffusion, specializing in specification model design, intelligent simulation system modeling, data modeling and analysis. In addition to having a strong background in operations management, mathematics, and statistics, I am also skilled in Java, Python, Matlab, and other popular programming languages. I also excels with ANYLOGIC simulation modeling software. During PhD period, I has authored and finished research for the National Social Science Foundation of China (18BJY066) and six academic articles.

Keywords: Energy economics, Complex system, Agent-based modeling, Operations management, Empirical studies
